press right arrow to run right

press left arrow to run left

press space to jump

How to play ?

First of all pick one of our four sonic characters, then pick the difficulty level you find most appropriate for you and finally let the game begin..
All you gotta do is run till the end line (yes it is that simple), but be careful of the incoming enemies if they hit you your health bar on the top of the left corner of the screen will decrease one life per hit. Five hits and you're done.

That's all have a nice game and may the odds be ever in your favour!
